Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save recipes to make later?
You can bookmark any recipe page in your browser, or use the print function to save a PDF copy. We also recommend using the Pinterest save button to add recipes to your Pinterest boards for easy access.
Can I print a recipe?
Yes! Every recipe page has a print button that opens a printer-friendly version of the recipe without ads or unnecessary elements. You can also use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P).
How do I adjust serving sizes?
Most of our recipes include a serving size adjuster. Simply click on the serving number and select your desired quantity - the ingredient amounts will automatically update.
Are nutritional values accurate?
Nutritional information is calculated using standard USDA database values and should be considered estimates. Actual values may vary based on specific ingredients and brands used.
Can I submit my own recipes?
We love hearing from our community! While we don't currently accept direct recipe submissions, feel free to share your ideas through our contact form and our team may develop a similar recipe.
How do I report an error in a recipe?
If you notice any errors in ingredients, instructions, or other recipe content, please contact us using the contact form. We appreciate your help in keeping our recipes accurate!
Can I use your recipes on my blog?
You may not republish our recipes in full. However, you're welcome to share a link to our recipes with a brief description (1-2 sentences) and a single photo with proper attribution.
How often do you add new recipes?
We add new recipes regularly, typically several times per week. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on Pinterest to stay updated with our latest creations.
